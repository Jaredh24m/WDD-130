Overview

Purpose: Something that stays with us forever are memories and our purpose is to fill your mind with memories, adventure and fun so you can spend them with your family and friends.

Audience: Who can come? The whole family is welcome, only children over 10 years old can board the boat. Can everyone practice rafting? If you have any medical restrictions or do not like adrenaline, then this sport is not for you, since there are places where the current is a little stronger. Do I need any preparation? No, you just need to bring a lot of desire to have fun, we have good instructors and guides who are trained for any occasion and can have a pleasant time
